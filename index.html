FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is arbitrage betting?
Arbitrage betting (or arbing) involves placing bets on all possible outcomes of an event across different bookmakers, where the combined odds guarantee a profit regardless of the result. The bot finds these opportunities automatically.
Is arbitrage betting legal?
Yes, arbitrage betting is completely legal. Some bookmakers may limit or close accounts of frequent arbers, but it is not against any law. We recommend spreading your activity across multiple bookmakers.
What do I need to get started?
You need a PC or Mac (Mac Big Sur or later, Windows 10 or later), a surebet.com premium account, Google Chrome, and a Telegram account. A full setup guide is included with every license.
Do I need to leave my computer on?
Yes, the bot runs on your PC or Mac so it needs to be left on and connected to the internet while the bot is running. It will alert you on your phone via Telegram wherever you are.
How quickly does it find bets?
The bot scans every 5 seconds and sends alerts instantly. Most bets are found and alerted within seconds of appearing on surebet.com.
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Each license is tied to one device for security. If you need to switch devices, simply contact us and we will reset your device registration free of charge.
Can I share it with friends?
Each license key only works on one device. If your friends want access they will need their own license key. Group deals may be available — contact us for details.
